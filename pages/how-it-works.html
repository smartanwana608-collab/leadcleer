How Leadcleer Works

Clean, filter, and organize your CSV files in just a few simple steps — directly in your browser.

1

Choose a Feature

Start by selecting one of Leadcleer’s tools from the Features page. Each tool is designed to solve a specific data problem.

  • Filter Real Estate Agents
  • Prompt-Based CSV Tool
2

Upload Your CSV File

Upload your CSV file directly into the browser. Your file is processed locally and is never stored on our servers.

Supported format: .csv

3

Run the Action

Depending on the tool, either:

  • Run an automated filter
  • Describe what you want to do in plain language

Leadcleer analyzes your data and applies the selected actions.

4

Review & Download

Preview the processed results, review row and column counts, and download your cleaned CSV instantly.

Your Data, Your Control

Leadcleer processes CSV files directly in your browser. We do not store, upload, or transmit your data.

You remain fully responsible for how the data is used and for compliance with applicable data protection laws.
